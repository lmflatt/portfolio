About me

I came to to web development from a career in music as a composer and an educator. It was hard to leave a career I loved and had studied so hard for, but writing code offers what really matters to me: challenging work that requires creativity and logical problem solving. I am highly motivated, and I genuinely enjoy learning new skills and expanding what I can accomplish.

I still practice music since it's gone from career to number one amateur passion. As a private teacher, I learned to play every wind instrument, piano, and even a little bit of guitar. My hands were a bit too large for the violin, but nobody's perfect. I also enjoyed a part-time career as a singer with the Nashville Opera during college. As a composer, I've written a large body of works for many types of ensemble, and I've spent endless hours arguing and lecturing about Bach and Chopin. You never have to hear it as long as I'm not forced to rebut ludicrous claims about a pop star.

Aside from those two main passions, I can hold my own in a game of golf or racquetball, and I love taking walks with my dog. I'm not a great athlete, but I love to move, play, and compete. And when I've moved enough, I'm still up for playing and competing in video games.

Play Me Inmore_vert

See More!

Info:close

Play Me In takes advantage of my background in composition. I wrote many short single-voice clips of music saved into separate wav files. They follow musical rules to make them all compatible and are organized into voice types such as melody, harmony, bass, percussion, etc. When the app runs, it makes random selections from the collections of clips to combine them into a single, full song. The wav files are reduced to byte arrays and combined into a new file which is then available for preview and dowload. Launched on AWS. Enjoy your new theme song!
